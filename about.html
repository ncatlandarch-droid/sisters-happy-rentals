Our Story

Two Sisters, One Dream

Sisters Happy Rentals was born from a simple belief: every celebration deserves to shine. Founded by sisters Nahama Green and Bianca Green right here in Greensboro, North Carolina, our company grew from a shared passion for bringing people together through beautiful, memorable events.

What started as helping friends and family decorate birthday parties and baby showers quickly blossomed into something bigger. We discovered that our combined eye for design, attention to detail, and genuine love for making people happy was exactly what the Triad needed.

Today, we're proud to be one of the Piedmont Triad's most trusted event décor and rental companies. From intimate backyard gatherings to grand celebrations, we pour our hearts into every event — because when you celebrate with Sisters Happy Rentals, you're celebrating with family.
