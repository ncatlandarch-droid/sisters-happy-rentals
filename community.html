Our Mission

Celebrations That Give Back

Every event we style helps create unforgettable moments for underserved youth in our community.

Why We Give

Every Child Deserves to Celebrate

At Sisters Happy Rentals, we believe that every young person deserves to feel special. Growing up in Greensboro, we saw firsthand how many young people in our community go without the celebrations that mark life's important moments — birthdays that go unnoticed, milestones that pass uncelebrated.

That's why we made a commitment: a portion of every event we style goes directly toward creating beautiful celebrations for underserved youth in the Piedmont Triad. Because when a child sits at a beautifully decorated table, sees balloons in their favorite colors, and feels truly celebrated — that memory lasts a lifetime.

When you book with Sisters Happy Rentals, you're not just planning your celebration — you're helping make someone else's dream celebration come true.

In Action

What Your Support Creates

Through our Give Back program, we've brought the full Sisters Happy Rentals experience to young people across the Triad — complete with balloon installations, themed décor, beautiful table settings, and photo backdrops.

We partner with local schools, community centers, foster care organizations, and youth programs to identify young people who could use a reason to smile. Then we do what we do best — create magic.

From prom send-offs for foster youth to birthday celebrations for children in transitional housing, every event is styled with the same love and care we bring to our paying clients. Because every child deserves to feel like royalty.
